# Iqama renewal dummy rules (illustrative demo data only)

All values below are fictional demo data — not real Saudi government
requirements. Always verify via Absher, Muqeem, or SADAD.

## Required documents (dummy)

1. Valid passport copy
2. Sponsor or employer letter confirming employment and sponsorship
3. Medical fitness certificate from an approved clinic

## Dummy renewal fee table (SAR)

| Item | Amount (SAR) |
|------|--------------|
| Primary holder renewal | 650 |
| Dependent renewal (each) | 650 |
| Optional express processing add-on | 100 |

## Grace-period rule (dummy)

- **Within 30 days of expiry:** standard renewal still applies; proceed with
  normal renewal steps (documents, medical check, fee payment draft) without a
  separate pre-step.
- **Beyond 30 days after expiry:** a different dummy corrective path may apply
  (e.g., exit-re-entry visa arrangement) before standard renewal is available
  again.

Example: expired 10 days ago → inside the 30-day grace window → standard
renewal applies.
